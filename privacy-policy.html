Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2026

Serenity Day Habilitation is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and protection of your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and business details provided during service signup or communication.
  • Payment Information: Credit card data, which is processed by third-party payment processors and is not stored by us directly.
  • Website Information: User-provided content, images, and materials for customization.
  • Technical Information: IP addresses, browser types, and access times collected automatically when you visit our website.
  • Cookies and Tracking: We use cookies and similar technologies to track user behavior and improve our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and customize our services per your specifications.
  • To communicate with you, respond to inquiries, and provide project updates.
  • To enhance and optimize our services.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. How We Share Your Information

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Third parties that assist with hosting, domains, and payment processing, who are contractually obligated to protect your data.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

4. Security of Your Information

We implement encryption, access controls, and regular security assessments to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

5. Your Choices and Rights

  • You may request access to or correction of your personal information by contacting us at 1-848-261-4875.
  • You may opt out of promotional communications at any time.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be communicated via email or a notice on our website.
