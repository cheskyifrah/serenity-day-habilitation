Who We Are

About Serenity Day Habilitation

Dedicated to building independence, confidence, and brighter futures for adults with developmental disabilities.

Our Mission

Building Skills, Strengthening Lives

At Serenity Day Habilitation, we provide a structured, supportive environment where adults with intellectual and developmental disabilities gain the life skills they need to live more independently. Our DDD/Medicaid-approved day habilitation programs combine hands-on learning, community engagement, and personalized goal-setting to help every individual move forward.

We believe every individual has untapped potential. Our dedicated team works hand-in-hand with participants and their families to design experiences that build real-world confidence — from mastering daily living skills to forming meaningful connections in the community.

With locations in Brick Township and West Long Branch, New Jersey, we serve families across Ocean and Monmouth counties with programs that create measurable, lasting progress.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

Compassion

We approach every interaction with empathy and genuine care, seeing each participant's strengths before their challenges.

Community

We foster real connections — between participants, families, staff, and the neighborhoods around us — because belonging fuels growth.

Empowerment

We don't do things for people — we equip them with the skills and confidence to do things for themselves.

Excellence

The Serenity Difference

What Makes Us Special

Individualized Care Plans

Every participant receives a personalized plan built around their strengths, interests, and goals — because meaningful progress starts with understanding the individual.

Experienced, Caring Staff

Our team brings professional training and genuine passion to every interaction, creating an environment where individuals feel safe to try, fail, and grow.

Family-Centered Approach

We keep families involved and informed at every step, because the best outcomes happen when everyone works together toward the same goals.

Engaging, Purposeful Programming

Our days are structured around activities that build real, transferable skills — not just filling time, but making every hour count toward greater independence.
